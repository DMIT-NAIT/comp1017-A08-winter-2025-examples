Edmonton Festivals

What's on in Edmonton

Summertime

International Street Performers Festival

The International Street Performers Festival is a summer staple in downtown Edmonton, featuring comedy, acrobatics, magic, dance, and music from global artists. It transforms city streets into lively stages where performers engage with audiences in a uniquely interactive way.

Open to all ages, the festival showcases seasoned and emerging talents, creating an inclusive space that celebrates street performance culture. It's a dynamic, family-friendly event, adding vibrancy and creativity to Edmonton's summer scene.

The Works Art & Design Festival

The Works Art & Design Festival turns downtown Edmonton into an outdoor gallery each summer, displaying a range of artworks from sculpture to digital media. For two weeks, the city hosts local and international artists who share diverse and innovative pieces with the public.

The festival promotes art appreciation with free workshops, tours, and talks, inviting visitors to engage directly with artists and their creations. It's a cultural highlight for art lovers and a cornerstone of Edmonton's creative community.

Taste of Edmonton

Taste of Edmonton is Alberta's largest food festival, offering a sampling of the city's culinary diversity in Sir Winston Churchill Square. Featuring local restaurants and food trucks, it's a favorite event for food lovers each summer.

Beyond food, the festival includes live entertainment, cooking demos, and tastings, creating a fun and flavorful experience for all ages. It's a celebration of Edmonton's food scene and a community gathering in the heart of the city.
